## Who Needs a Commercial License?

You may need a commercial license if:

- **You modify Synco and deploy it as a hosted service** but cannot or do not want to release your modifications under AGPL-3.0.
- **You embed Synco in a proprietary product** and cannot comply with AGPL-3.0 copyleft requirements.
- **Your organization's legal policy prohibits AGPL dependencies** in your software stack.

You do **not** need a commercial license if:

- You use Synco unmodified for personal or household use.
- You self-host Synco and comply with the AGPL-3.0 license terms.
- You contribute modifications back to the project under AGPL-3.0.
